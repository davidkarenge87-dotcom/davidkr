About Us

The Sport Department is responsible for developing sports talent, organizing competitions, and promoting physical fitness among students. We believe in discipline, teamwork, and excellence.

Our Sports

Football

Training and competitive matches for both men and women.

Basketball

Skill development, leagues, and inter-university tournaments.

Athletics

Track and field events including sprints, jumps, and throws.

Hockey

Field hockey training focusing on speed, skill, and teamwork.

Rugby

Strength, strategy, and competitive rugby matches.

Tennis

Singles and doubles training for recreational and competitive players.

Lacrosse

Fast-paced lacrosse sessions focusing on coordination and endurance.

Badminton

Indoor badminton training emphasizing agility and reflexes.
